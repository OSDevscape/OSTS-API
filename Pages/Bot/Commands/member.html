Member Commands

Member commands are available to regular clan members and cover economy, progression, utility, and gameplay features.

Economy

Currency, banking, gambling, and sales commands.

  • !atm – Check your personal balance.
  • !coffer – Daily clan coffer contribution reward.
  • !dep – Deposit money into your account.
  • !dink – Fun social currency interaction.
  • !fordink – Another social‑economy command.
  • !lb – View the clan economy leaderboard.
  • !rob – Attempt to steal from another member.
  • !rr – Russian roulette‑style gamble.
  • !sell – Sell items for currency.
  • !slots – Spin the slots for a chance at rewards.
  • !store – Open the clan store interface.
  • !wd – Withdraw money from your account.

Progression

Leveling, skills, quests, and recurring tasks.

  • !level – Check your current bot level or XP tier.
  • !quest – Monthly quest‑style objective and reward.
  • !raid – Weekly raid objective and reward.
  • !skillsxp – View experience gains by skill.
  • !task – Hourly task objective and reward.

Utility

Info, profiles, tags, and convenience tools.

  • !apply – Submit an application to join the clan.
  • !botinfo – Show information about the bot.
  • !entry – View a stored log or entry for your account.
  • !stats – View your overall stats summary.
  • !time – Check the server or clan time.
  • !vote – Get the link to vote for the bot.
  • !create-tag – Create a custom saved response tag.
  • !remove-tag – Delete one of your custom tags.
  • !tag – Send a saved tag response.
  • !taglist – List available tags.

Gameplay

Active resource gathering and inventory management.

  • !chop – Chop logs to gather resources.
  • !fish – Catch fish as a resource activity.
  • !inv – View your current inventory.
  • !mine – Mine ores to gather resources.

Slayer Diaries slash commands

Slash commands that integrate with the OSTS Slayer Diaries API.

  • /slayer – Search The Slayer Diaries for a specific Slayer monster.
  • /entry – View a detailed entry from The Slayer Diaries.
