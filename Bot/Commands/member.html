Member Commands

Member commands are available to regular clan members and friends who use the bot every day.

Slayer Diaries

Lookup tools for The Slayer Diaries monster compendium.

  • /slayer – Search The Slayer Diaries for a specific Slayer monster.
  • /entry – View a detailed entry from The Slayer Diaries.

Economy

Currency, banking, gambling, and sales commands.

  • !atm – Open the clan ATM to manage your funds.
  • !dep – Deposit coins into your in‑bot bank balance.
  • !lb – View the clan economy leaderboard.
  • !rob – Attempt to rob another member for coins.
  • !rr – Play Russian roulette style coin gamble.
  • !sell – Sell items for coins.
  • !slots – Spin the slot machine for a chance to win.
  • !store – Open the in‑bot store to browse items.
  • !wd – Withdraw coins from your in‑bot bank.

Progression

Hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly rewards plus progression tools.

  • !coffer – Claim your daily reward (coffer).
  • !quest – Claim your monthly reward (quest).
  • !raid – Claim your weekly reward (raid).
  • !task – Claim your hourly reward (task).
  • !skillsxp – Check your skills experience progress.

Utility

Info, profiles, convenience, and social tools.

  • !botinfo – View information and stats about the bot.
  • !dink – Use the dink social/fun utility command.
  • !fordink – Manage or interact with dink‑related features.
  • !time – Show the current server or bot time.
  • !vote – Get the vote link or confirm your vote status.

Gameplay

Active resource gathering and inventory management.

  • !chop – Chop logs to gather resources.
  • !fish – Catch fish as a resource activity.
  • !inv – View your current inventory.
  • !mine – Mine ores to gather resources.
