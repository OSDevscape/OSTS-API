Member Commands

Member commands are available to regular clan members and friends who use the bot every day.

General utility

  • /help – Show a categorized list of available commands.
  • /profile – View your clan profile, ranks, and basic stats.
  • /links – Display useful links like the website, API, and wiki.

Clan & Slayer tools

  • /slayer – Look up Slayer Diary entries or monster info.
  • /task – Show details about your current Slayer task (if supported).
  • /rank – Check your clan rank and progression.

Economy & fun

  • /balance – View your in‑bot currency balance.
  • /daily – Claim a daily reward or stipend.
  • /shop – Browse items available in the clan shop.
