Guest Commands

Guest commands are available to visitors and new members so they can explore the clan and the bot safely.

Getting to know the clan

  • /info – Learn about the clan, rules, and basic requirements.
  • /events – View upcoming clan events or activities.
  • /apply – Get information on how to apply or join.

Bot & support

  • /bot – Show a short description of what Old School Tool Set can do.
  • /support – Get an invite to the support server.
  • /ping – Check if the bot is online and see latency.
