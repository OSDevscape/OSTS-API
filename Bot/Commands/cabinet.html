Cabinet Commands

Cabinet members handle most day‑to‑day moderation and clan management tasks.

Moderation

  • !warn <user> [reason] – Issue a warning to a member.
  • !mute <user> [duration] – Temporarily mute a member.
  • !kick <user> [reason] – Remove a user from the server.
  • !ban <user> [reason] – Ban a user from the server.

Channel tools

  • .tidy <amount> – Bulk delete messages in the current channel.
